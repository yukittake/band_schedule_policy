Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to the Service Provider or to third parties.

Users may enter band member names and schedule information into the Application. This information is stored locally on the user’s device only and is not shared, transmitted, or otherwise made accessible to the Service Provider or to third parties.

The Application does not gather precise location data, nor does it collect network identifiers such as IP addresses or analytics about your usage.

Children

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since all data is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted externally, children’s information is not accessible to the Service Provider. If you believe a child has provided personally identifiable information to the Service Provider through any means, please contact the Service Provider so that necessary actions can be taken.

Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, the confidentiality of your information depends on your device’s security features (such as operating system protections and device access controls). The Service Provider does not receive or store any user data.
